About

I build things.

Software, infrastructure, physical objects — whatever the problem calls for. My background spans design, carpentry (both in commercial film settings and large scale construction), and over 15 years of security, operations, and infrastructure engineering at companies such as ExpressVPN, Facebook/Meta, and GoDaddy.

Each role shaped how I think about structure, materials, and failure modes — whether that's a dovetail joint, a network perimeter, or an iOS app architectural decision.

The projects on this site (some OSS, some Commercial) are what happens when the ideas that have accumulated over a long period of time finally get built — illustrating an attempt at thoughtful design, honest engineering, and security where it matters.
